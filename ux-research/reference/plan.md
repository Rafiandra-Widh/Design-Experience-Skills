# Mode: plan — Research Planning

Produce study materials: research plan, screener, interview guide, usability test protocol.

## Intake

- What decision will this research inform? What does the team already believe (hypotheses)?
- Method preference, or should you recommend one? Match method to question:
  - *Why/how do users think X?* → semi-structured interviews
  - *Can users complete X?* → usability test
  - *Which option?* → preference/comparative test
  - *How many/how often?* → survey or analytics, not interviews
- Participants: who, how many (5–8 per segment for qual), how they'll be recruited.
- Logistics: remote/in-person, moderated/unmoderated, session length, timeline, incentive.

## Research plan template

```
# Research Plan: [judul]
**Latar belakang** — konteks & keputusan yang akan didukung
**Pertanyaan riset** — 3–5 pertanyaan (BUKAN pertanyaan interview; ini yang tim ingin tahu)
**Hipotesis** — apa yang tim yakini sekarang + bagaimana bisa terbukti salah
**Metode & alasan**
**Partisipan** — kriteria inklusi/eksklusi, jumlah, rekrutmen
**Timeline & peran** — moderator, notulen, observer
**Output** — bentuk deliverable & kapan
```

## Screener rules

- Screen for *behavior* ("Kapan terakhir kali Anda ...?"), never intent ("Apakah Anda mau ...?").
- Include disqualifiers (works in UX/market research; used the product's competitor team; participated in research < 6 months ago — adjust per study).
- Multiple-choice with the qualifying answer NOT guessable from question order.

## Interview guide rules

- Funnel structure: warm-up → general behavior → specific topic → deep dives → wrap-up.
- Open, past-tense, non-leading: "Ceritakan terakhir kali Anda ..." — never "Apakah Anda suka fitur X?"
- No feature-validation questions disguised as research ("Would you use...?" is banned).
- Include probes under each main question ("Mengapa?", "Lalu apa yang terjadi?", "Bisa beri contoh?").
- 45–60 min guide = ~6–8 main questions max.

## Usability test protocol rules

- Tasks are *scenarios with a goal*, never instructions: "Anda ingin tahu berapa pengeluaran bulan lalu" — not "Klik menu Laporan".
- Per task define: starting point, success criteria (observable), and metrics (completion, errors, time, SEQ 1–7 after each task; SUS at the end if benchmarking).
- Script the moderator's neutral prompts ("Apa yang Anda pikirkan sekarang?") and the think-aloud instruction.
- Pilot with 1 person before the real sessions — state this in the plan.
